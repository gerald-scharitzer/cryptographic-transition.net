# The Cryptographic Transition

We replace the fundamental algorithms, the algorithmic foundation, that run our trust-based systems.

Our modern information society runs on rational and reasonable trust in public key cryptography.

Efficient quantum computers could affect the foundations of this trust and thus trust itself.

We better protect the trust in our systems with quantum-safe cryptography
by utilizing both hybrid classic and quantum-safe algorithms,
like lattice- or hash-based cryptography.

To know what is out there requires a cryptographic inventory.

To know where to do what requires cryptographic awareness.

To actually do it requires cryptographic agility.
